## Our Mission

QAStudy.online is an educational platform built for those who want to build a successful career in software quality assurance. We believe that quality testing is the foundation of reliable and secure software products.

## What We Offer

Our courses cover the full spectrum of QA testing — from manual testing for beginners to advanced automation and security testing. Each course is designed by practicing professionals with real-world experience at top technology companies.
